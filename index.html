LiteEmbed improves classification, retrieval, segmentation, detection, and generation without retraining CLIP.

CLIP Struggles on Rare Classes

While CLIP achieves strong zero-shot performance, it fails on underrepresented and culturally specific classes. Many classes receive near-zero recognition accuracy.

Why Naïve Optimization Fails

Naïve alignment causes discriminative collapse and semantic drift. LiteEmbed restores structure.

Key Insight: Hierarchical CLIP Geometry

High-variance components capture coarse semantics. Low-variance components encode fine-grained distinctions.
